Under Pinnocchio's Nose

Pinocchio’s Children Library is a multi-layered project which combines a kids’ library with a superlocal landmark and a park, part of the European Fairytale Route. Pinocchio’s adventures is a classic fairytale and his nose is its signature image. We used the truncated cone form to interpret this image and host the main library facilities. The cones serve as reading rooms, team work space, music and story telling rooms. Reading and discovering under... Pinocchio’s nose! The already existing factory works as Geppetto’s Workshop with creative recreation boothsand a puppet theatre. Walking through the “noses” one reaches the existing Pinocchio’s Park, across the river, where the Auditorium and the Cultural Centre of Carlo Collodi, Pinocchio’s writer are located.

In collaboration with Natalia Kogia and Stylianos Tsaparas
