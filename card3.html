Primary School in Mozambique

Beehive aims to create a friendly space for every child and adult of the local community. It is an educational centre where disabled children can safely play, communicate and integrate into society. Beehive consists of four main areas: Education area, made up of the classrooms, the covered playground and an activities room. Free time area, consisting of the dining area and the kitchen pantry, the multipurpose space and the open kitchen. Administration area, containing the teachers’ room, the management and principal offices, storage rooms and an enlarged infirmary. Interaction open area, with open playground, trees and sitting, where students, teachers and parents can interact with each other.

In collaboration with Eleni Ntouni
