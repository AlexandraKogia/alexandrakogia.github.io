Office Party @ Bristol Light Festival

Office Party is a project designed by Parker Heyl and Michael Wagner, exhibited in the Bristol Light Festival in March 2022.

Involvement: Part of the set up, installation, photoshooting and maintainance crew.

The installation comes from the playful idea that our workspaces may come to life in our absence. By using networked kinetic technologies, Office Party choreographs a performance in the empty offices of the city. Office blinds are a banal fixture, and their unexpected liveliness allows this art installation to create a moment of magic.
