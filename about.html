Alexandra is a London based architect and experience designer, coming from Greece. She is passionate about exploring new ways of designing and experiencing space. Because of her architecture-engineering degree, she has experience in conceptual and structural design, concerning different scales, subjects and materials.

After getting her degree as an architect-engineer from the National Technical University of Athens, she has worked as a junior architect and construction manager in Athens and Santorini. Studying at Bartlett, UCL, MArch of Design for Performance and Interaction gave her the chance to work on multidisciplinary and multimedia interactive projects.

She conceives interaction as the main idea of architecture, as every moment one stands, moves and acts in a built or unbuilt environment there is an interaction taking place. She strongly believes that the extraordinary is hidden in the ordinary and waits to be explored...
