The Court of Miracles

The court of Miracles is a scenography installation project, located in the refugee's social housing blocks in Athens. The atmosphere of the place fits the character of the play and is enriched with light structures, which serve the act. the plot takes place in the 1970s, in a poor Athenian neighbourhood and the proposal aims to introduce the audience to that era.

A project in collaboration with Constantinos Grammenidis.
