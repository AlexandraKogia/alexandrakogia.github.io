Co-Blue

In these difficult times of the Pandemic, more and more people experience Coronavirus Blues. Their friends and loved ones are those who can help overcome this sad situation.

We created a multiplayer game in Unity based on this idea. Our hero experiences different stages of coronavirus blues, depression and abuses, but their friends help him balance and rehab!

A project in collaboration with Jee Eun Lee, Yong Won Choi and Sau Man Cho, for M.Arch of Design for Performance and Interaction, Bartlett, UCL, 2020-2021
